ACCOMMODATION


Be ready to take a sneak peak into some of the most luxurious Rooms & Suites in the world.
Experience it all at your stay at Santani!



The Santani King Suite

Our luxurious Santani King Suite offers a contemporary design with breathtaking views that bless the eyes of all visitors. The suite comprises of a large King bed, a spacious own private terrace, and a separate elegant living room area along with the 24-carat gold-plated Sherle Wagner fixtures,as well as hand-crafted white marble vanities.


Empire State Penthouse Suite

Our glamourous penthouse offers a magnificent seaside view. Accessed via a private lift, this outstanding split level penthouse features several balconies & a private rooftop terrace overlooking the sea. There is also a vast living room, a dining room, a gym, a spacious bedroom with a large en-suite marble bathroom, a steam room and two walk-in wardrobes.


The Royal Queen Suite

A lavishly appointed, three-bedroom suite providing private elevator access, specially designed for large families and those seeking to entertain themselves in grand style. The spacious dressing area with leather and wood paneled luxury closets is known to be one of the best attractionsin this suite. This also consists of a master bedroom featuring mosaic floors and walls with a gilded floral motif.
